Lean on Me

We Make Your Wedding Experience a Reflection of Your Love Story and Values

At Lean On Me Events, we understand your need to know that your wedding is in capable and reliable hands. We also understand your desire to have your vision and hopes truly known.

We make sure your wedding bursts with fullness, but it’s not about the over-the-top arrangements or extravagant drapery and lighting (although that is important). Your wedding truly comes to life, because you the couple are our muse, and creative artistry and organization are our intuitive and learned gifts.

We’ve been handcrafting well-orchestrated weddings and serving heart-intertwined couples since 2011. In those years, we’ve learned that as much as your wedding is about the experience of uniting in front of family and friends, it’s also about spending time with those loved ones from the moment one short, but powerful question sets things in motion.

We offer month-of, partial planning, and full-service planning and design packages. If you’re a gal who’s enlisted an entourage of helpers, we work with you, but we also help you put yourself in each person’s shoes to make sure you make the right decision for your wedding day.

Explore our services page to discover how we can serve you on your wedding day, so you’re at ease when you step into your dress and every moment before and after.
