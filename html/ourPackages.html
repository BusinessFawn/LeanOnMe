Here’s how we serve you:

Full-Service Wedding Planning and Design

Brides with bursting schedules who know they need wedding planning support from start to finish are the perfect fit for our full-service package. We help you book all of your must-haves with vendors who match your wedding wishes to a ‘T’ and help you stay on budget. You remain in control and are informed with up-to-date information through our online planning portal and regular contact with us. You receive style boards and customized design based on your creative sessions with us, so your wedding vision becomes a remarkable realty. From researching and setting up meetings with vendors to preparing guest welcome bags, we make sure your wedding vision becomes an experience you and your fiancé remember for the rest of your lives.

Partial Planning: 6 months and 3 months

Brides who dance the line between DIY and wanting an expert’s guidance and support love our partial planning package. Partial planning begins 6 months or 3 months leading up to your wedding and includes vendor research for 1 or 3 vendor categories(depending on the package), style board concepts to help you create a cohesive design, timelines and diagrams, wedding day production, and more.

Month-of Coordination

For Type A list makers and go-getters who want to pass the torch on their wedding day, month of coordination is a wonderful fit. The month before your wedding, you receive assistance tying up loose ends and finalizing your headcount. We create wedding day timelines and diagrams as well as provide a lead wedding planner and assistant for rehearsal dinner and wedding day production. You plan your wedding and we make sure you and your family get to enjoy it without having to run ragged or stress about who and what goes where and when.
