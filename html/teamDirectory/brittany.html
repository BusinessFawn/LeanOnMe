From the Bottom of My Heart, I Love to Make Your Wedding Dreams Come True

This moment in time is soooo surreal, isn’t it? The love of your life has just asked you to spend a lifetime side-by-side in marriage. It makes me so happy to think of all the excitement that’s running through your mind and heart right now.

I don’t want your wedding to-do list to sweep in and steal that joy. I’d rather you spend more time falling even deeper in love every single day than falling into a state of frenzy over all that needs to come together.

That’s my job, and I love it! My heart bursts a little each time I think about the opportunity I have to make your wedding dreams come true.

Hi lovebug, I’m Brittany!

Just think…once you get to the end of the aisle, you get to be with the man you’ve dreamed of. I make sure you get to focus on that instead of being preoccupied with vendor setup, timelines, or whether your centerpieces are just right.

I love big, laugh big, and serve big, which is exactly why planning weddings is the perfect fit. In my free time, you’ll find me volunteering at my church or spending quality time with family and friends. I laugh at just about anything and always try to keep things lighthearted and fun.
